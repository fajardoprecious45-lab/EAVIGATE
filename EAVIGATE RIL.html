📚 About Emilio Aguinaldo College – Cavite Campus

Emilio Aguinaldo College - Cavite Campus is a private, non-sectarian, co-educational higher education institution located in Burol Main, Dasmariñas City, Cavite, Philippines. It is one of the two main campuses of Emilio Aguinaldo College (the other being in Manila) and serves a large and diverse student community. The Cavite campus has grown significantly since its establishment in 1996 and now offers a broad range of academic programs and facilities for senior high school, undergraduate, and graduate students.

🎓 Academic Programs

Senior High School: Academic Tracks: ABM, STEM, HumSS | Technical-Vocational Tracks: ICT, Culinary, Tourism, Automotive, Electronics, and more

Undergraduate Degrees: Business and Management, Criminology, Nursing and Health Sciences, Engineering and Technology, Psychology and Humanities, Law (Juris Doctor) — the Campus' own law school, one of the first in Cavite

Graduate Studies: Advanced programs in education, criminal justice, nursing, and related fields

🏗️ Campus Facilities

The campus features multiple academic buildings, a library, science and computer labs, a covered court, sports oval, and student-friendly spaces. EAC Cavite continually expands infrastructure to accommodate a growing student population and new program offerings.

👥 Student Life & Community

EAC Cavite fosters a supportive learning environment with active student organizations, academic support services, and preparation for professional careers. It emphasizes character development, community involvement, and real-world experience through internships and partnerships with local industries and healthcare facilities.

📝 Admissions

Prospective students can apply online or visit the admissions office to start enrollment. EAC Cavite welcomes fresh high school graduates, transfer students, and those seeking to pursue college degrees or graduate studies.

♿ EAC Cavite – Accessibility Route Summary

EAC Cavite ensures a safe and inclusive campus with barrier-free access to classrooms, offices, and facilities for Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) and visitors with mobility needs.

🚪 Accessible Entrances & Lobbies

  • Entrance Lobby (Building 1 – Ground Floor)
  • Lobby 1 – Activity Center
  • Lobby 2 – Parking Area
  • Lobby 3
  • All lobbies provide wheelchair access and wide pathways

🏫 Accessible Classrooms

Building 1: Ground Floor: 1101, 1108, 1109, 1110
Building 2: Second Floor: 2201-2210 | Third Floor: 2301, 2307, 2308, 2309
Building 4: Second Floor: 4202, 4204, 4206
Building 5: Second Floor: 5201-5210
Building 3 & Extension: Ground Floor: 3102-B, 3105 | Extension: 3E104, 3E105, 3E106

🚻 Accessible Comfort Rooms

  • Building 2: Ground, Second, and Third Floors
  • Building 4: Ground & Second Floors
  • Building 5: Ground & Second Floors
  • Building 3 Extension: Ground Floor

🏢 Accessible Offices & Support

  • Office of Student Affairs (Bldg 1 – Ground Floor)
  • Students Recruitment & Admissions (Bldg 1 – Ground Floor)
  • Accounting Office, Registrar, Credit & Collection (Bldg 2 – Ground Floor)
  • Guidance Office (Bldg 2 – Ground Floor)
  • Deans, President, and VP Offices (Bldg 1 – Second Floor)

🚗 Parking & Routes

  • Lobby 2 – Parking Area: PWD-friendly parking & drop-off
  • Wide pathways & ramps connecting major academic, administrative, and support facilities

✅ Summary: All main lobbies are barrier-free. Ground floors and key classrooms/offices are accessible. Comfort rooms and health/counseling services are accessible. Ramps, wide corridors, and clear pathways ensure smooth mobility across campus.
